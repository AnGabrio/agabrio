What is Bayesian inference?

What is probability ? The answer to this question is generally acknowledged to be the one that respects the so called Kolmogorov axioms which can be brutally simplified to: Probabilities are non-negative Probabilities sum to one The joint probability of disjoint events is the sum of the probabilities of the events One of the ways in which Bayesian statistics differs from classical statistics is in the interpretation of probability.

The moment for the second edition of the HEART’s one-day introductory course to health economics arrived at last!
